Osso Buco

Osso Buco stuffing recipes are endlessly customizable. All you have to do is change up the bread, the herbs, and/or the liquid. But if a classic recipe is what you’re after, you’ve found it, though even this one can be tweaked for your taste. For a less rich stuffing, omit the last 4 Tbs. of melted butter and add a bit more stock.
